List of utilities

Haversine Distance

The haversine formula determines the great-circle distance between two points on a sphere given their longitudes and latitudes.

GeoCoder/Reverse GeoCoder

Geocoder is a feature, which tells you the location of a place. Reverse Geocode is a feature, which tells you the address of the given lat-lng.

Bearing Angle

The true bearing to a point is the angle measured in degrees in a clockwise direction from the north line. We will refer to the true bearing simply as the bearing.

Show Me Route!

In this feature, you can see a route from a source to destination that you have selected. Try to give more precise name of the place
